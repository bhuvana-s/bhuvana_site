Speaker Profile

Short Profile

Bhuvaneswari Subramani is Chielf Cloud Evangelist at Intuitive.Cloud, a technophile on a continuous pursuit to unlearn, learn and share. She has vast experience in Information Technology with leadership in implementing the right processes, teams, and tools to devise cloud modernisation strategies for complex legacy and on-premise applications. She has significantly improved engineering outcomes through innovation focusing on product development, designing microservices, and architecting and implementing business-critical distributed applications.

Lead Cloud Engineering and Operations, Thought Leadership creation on cloud migration/modernization solutons & strategies, foster alliance partnerships to evangelize cloud, and collaborate with Sales Leaders to drive cloud adoption

She is passionate Tech Blogger with over a decade of experience in writing and publishing articles on DevOps & Cloud Computing and mentoring teams and communities.

She is an active speaker on Technology and Leadership in AWS Communities, international conferences, TEDx and universities. Won honorary awards including WomenOfInfor, AWS Rockstar of Infor from Infor, AWS Hero, AWS Hero of the Year 2022, and Now Go Build 2022 from AWS CTO (Werner Vogels) etc
